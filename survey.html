Petition To Expand Shuttle Bus Service to KPU Richmond Campus

Page 1 of 17

What is the Problem: Students at Kwantlen Polytechnic University currently face limited shuttle bus options connection, Surrey, Langley, and Cloverdale campuses with the Richmond campus. This creates barriers to accessibility, safety, and academic participation, particularly to those with evening classes and late study or work schedules.

What we are doing about this problem: We, the undersigned, respectfully petition the KPU Multipass Committee and the KPU Student Association to expand shuttle bus service between Surrey, Langley, and Cloverdale campuses and the Richmond campus, operating from 8:00am to 11:00pm, Monday through Friday.

Why does this matter:

  • Improve accessibility and equity for students across all campuses.
  • Enhance safety for students travelling at night.
  • Reduce reliance on cars and support KPU’s sustainability goals.

Our Call to Action: We urge the KPU Multipass Committee and KPU Student Association to take immediate steps to review, consider, and implement expanded shuttle services for the benefit of the student body.
